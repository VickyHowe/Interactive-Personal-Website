About Me

Since I was little, I have always loved making things work, which led me into software development to deepen my understanding and drive improvements. With over 10 years of retail business ownership and 8 years in electronic product design and documentation, I bring a holistic approach to problem-solving. My journey from business ownership and designing electronic systems to crafting software solutions equips me with a comprehensive understanding of technology and its impact on business success.

I am dedicated to leveraging my diverse skill set to create innovative, efficient, and user-centric software that drives growth and enhances productivity. With strong communication skills, attention to detail, time management, and adaptability, I am excited to tackle the challenges in software development. I have a passion for learning and staying up-to-date with rapidly changing technologies, always looking for opportunities to apply my skills to real-world applications.

As an experienced hardware technologist and former business owner, I bring a unique perspective to any challenge. Let's transform ideas into reality with a seamless integration of business acumen, electronic innovation, and software excellence. Outside of work, you can find me enjoying outdoor activities like skiing and hiking, indulging in hobbies like soap making and video games, and spending time in the garden."
