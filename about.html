About Me

Since I was little, I always love making things work. My journey into software development seemed like a natural progression to better understand how things work and how I can improve upon them.

With over 10 years in retail business ownership and 8 years in electronic product design and documentation employing strong communication skills, attention to detail, time management and adaptability I am excited to tackle the challenges in software development.

I have a passion for learning, and staying UpToDate with rapidly changing technologies. Looking at full systems and breaking them down is a passion of mine. I am looking for more opportunities to apply my skills to real world applications. As an experienced hardware technologist and former business owner I bring a unique perspective to any challenge.

If I'm not studying you can find me outside in the mountains skiing or hiking. I also enjoy making soap, video games, and time out in the garden.
